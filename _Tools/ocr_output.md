traffic signals but doesn’t manufacture. So we will need to size the installations and

Have you thought about why the case is asking for Manhattan specifically? The answer
is because it’s easy to determine the number of traffic signal systems since Manhattan is
basically a grid. So the First step is to determine the number of traffic signal systems

e Assumptions: Traffic signal systems last 10 years
