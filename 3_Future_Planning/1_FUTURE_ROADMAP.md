- [ ] **Financial Runway Check:** Determine how many months of runway I have.

### 2. The Strategic Fork (Week 2)
**Decision:** Do I seek a High-Leverage Job or Start a New Venture?

#### Option A: High-Leverage Job (Chief of Staff, Head of Growth, Founder's Office)
*   *Pros:* Immediate income, network expansion, learning on someone else's dime.
*   *Target Companies:* Series A/B startups, VC firms, Scale-ups.
*   *Action:* Build a "Target 50" list of companies.

#### Option B: New Venture (Founder)
*   *Pros:* Full control, high upside, utilizing the "Co-Founder" momentum.
*   *Cons:* High risk, requires capital/fundraising.
*   *Action:* Validate 3 new ideas.
