# CONTEXT FOR FUTURE PLAN: The "Pairwire" Chapter

This folder contains the complete "toolkit" derived from your experience at Pairwire. It is organized to help you in your future career, startups, and legal protection.

## 📂 Folder Structure

### **0_MASTER_PLAN.md**
*   **What it is:** The high-level summary of this entire experience.
*   **Use it for:** Reminding yourself of the "big picture" narrative and key wins.

### **1_FUTURE_ROADMAP.md**
*   **What it is:** The strategic plan for the next 6-12 months.
*   **Use it for:** Tracking your progress on the "Pivot" (Job vs. Startup) and executing the campaign.

### **1_Evidence_and_Proof/**
*   *Contains:* `EVIDENCE_COMPLETE.md`, `COFOUNDER_PROOF.md`, `ORIGINAL_CONTEXT_SUMMARY.txt`

*   **Key File:** `COFOUNDER_PROOF.md` is the "smoking gun" document.

### **2_Legal_and_Contracts/**
*   *Contains:* `CONTRACT_ANALYSIS.md`, `DRAFT_RSPA_TEMPLATE.txt`
*   **Purpose:** Your legal education.
*   **Key Lesson:** `DRAFT_RSPA_TEMPLATE.txt` is a good example of what a *real* founder agreement looks like (Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement). Use it as a reference for your next company.

### **3_Negotiation_Tactics/**
*   *Contains:* `NEGOTIATION_STRATEGY.md`, `SUCCESSFUL_NEGOTIATION_EMAIL_TEMPLATE.md`, `REALITY_CHECK.md`

*   **Key File:** `SUCCESSFUL_NEGOTIATION_EMAIL_TEMPLATE.md` is the exact email that secured your exit. Use its structure (Context -> Leverage -> Conditions) for future high-stakes negotiations.

### **4_Career_and_Narrative/**
*   *Contains:* `CAREER_RECOVERY_STRATEGY.md`, `NARRATIVE_SCRIPT.md`, `PITCH_ASSETS.md`, `Personal_Artifacts/`
*   **Purpose:** Selling this experience to the world.
*   **Key File:** `NARRATIVE_SCRIPT.md` tells you exactly what to say to interviewers to turn this "failure" into a "strategic exit."

### **5_Future_Startup_Playbook/**
*   *Contains:* `STARTUP_PLAYBOOK.md`
*   **Purpose:** The rules for your next venture.
*   **Key Lesson:** "Paper First." Never work without a signed contract.
