THE DIMENSIONS ASSESSED: PROBLEM-SOLVING
DIMENSION
DESCRIPTION
Structuring
• Identify client’s goal and question to solve; clarify ambiguities
• Propose a structure composed of a comprehensive set of independent drivers
• Ensure that approach is prioritized and insightful, and that it will solve the case
Math
• Lay out a clear and efficient approach
• Calculate correctly, quickly, and confidently, without help
• State implications
Judgement and insights
• Process new information quickly, prioritizing what is important
• Connect findings to develop sound recommendations
• Share deep insights and far-reaching implications
Synthesis
• Make a sound and practical recommendation in a convincing way
• Support recommendation with key points, followed by specific next steps
Case leadership
• Progress the case quickly, staying focused on the case objectives
• Ask probing questions, make reasonable assumptions, and request missing information
• Build on new findings to develop recommendation, adapt approach, and suggest next 
steps
Creativity
• Share numerous, varied, and sound ideas in a structured way
