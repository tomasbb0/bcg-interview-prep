## ⚡ KEY PRINCIPLES

1. **Structure > Knowledge**: BCG cares more about HOW you think than WHAT you know
2. **2-minute rule**: Your initial framework should take MAX 2 minutes to present
3. **Hypothesis-driven**: Always state a hypothesis before diving into analysis
4. **Synthesis often**: After each analysis section, summarize before moving on
5. **Portuguese natural**: Don't translate from English in your head — practice thinking in Portuguese
6. **MECE everything**: Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive — the BCG bible
7. **Numbers matter**: Always sanity-check your math. Round aggressively.
8. **End strong**: Your final recommendation should be clear, structured (3 points max), and confident
